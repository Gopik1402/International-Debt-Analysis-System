The 'debt spread' shows massive volatility, highlighting years of heavy borrowing followed by debt restructuring.

Cumulative rolling sums show that national debt almost never decreases, growing aggressively year-over-year.

Broad aggregated indicators (like Total DOD) far exceed the mathematical average of highly granular, specific indicators.

9. Identify countries contributing more than 5% of global debt.
